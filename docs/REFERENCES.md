# References

The literature that informed this project's kinematics analysis. The PDFs that were
previously vendored under `Articles/` have been removed from the repository (they are
copyrighted); this file lists them with full citations and links so they can be located
from their original sources.

## Project dissertations (primary sources)

These MSc dissertations are the origin of this work:

1. **J. Cunha**, *A Collaborative Work Cell to Improve Ergonomics and Productivity*,
   MSc dissertation, University of Minho, 2021.
2. **J. Pereira**, *Human-Like Motion Generation through Waypoints for Collaborative Robots
   in Industry 4.0*, MSc dissertation, University of Minho, 2021.

## UR / 6R kinematics papers (formerly in `Articles/`)

3. **K. P. Hawkins**, *Analytic Inverse Kinematics for the Universal Robots UR-5/UR-10
   Arms*, Technical Report, Georgia Institute of Technology, 2013.
   <https://repository.gatech.edu/entities/publication/e56759bc-92c8-43df-aa62-0dc47581459d>
   (PDF: <https://smartech.gatech.edu/bitstream/handle/1853/50782/ur_kin_tech_report_1.pdf>)

4. **R. S. Andersen**, *Kinematics of a UR5*, Technical Report, Aalborg University,
   31 May 2018. <https://rasmusan.blog.aau.dk/files/ur5_kinematics.pdf>

5. **S. Chen, M. Luo, O. Abdelaziz, and G. Jiang**, *A general analytical algorithm for
   collaborative robot (cobot) with 6 degree of freedom (DOF)*, in Proc. 2017
   International Conference on Applied System Innovation (ICASI), IEEE, 2017.
   DOI: [10.1109/ICASI.2017.7988522](https://doi.org/10.1109/ICASI.2017.7988522) —
   <https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/7988522/>

6. **A. Ahmad et al.**, *Multiple configurations for puncturing robot positioning*,
   Journal of Advances in Robotics & Expert Systems (JARES); also arXiv:1903.02281, 2019.
   <https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.02281> —
   PDF: <https://airccse.com/jares/papers/1419jares01.pdf>

7. *Inverse kinematic solution of 6R robot* — general 6R manipulator inverse-kinematics
   reference. A definitive publication record for the exact PDF that was vendored could
   not be reconfirmed; the topic is covered comprehensively by refs. 3–5 and 8. Search:
   <https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=inverse+kinematic+solution+of+6R+robot>

8. *Real-Time Human-Robot Interactions and Speed Control of a Robotic Arm for
   Collaborative Operations* — background on collaborative-operation speed/separation
   control (context, not kinematics). A definitive DOI for the exact PDF that was vendored
   could not be reconfirmed. Search:
   <https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=Real-Time+Human-Robot+Interactions+and+Speed+Control+of+a+Robotic+Arm+for+Collaborative+Operations>
