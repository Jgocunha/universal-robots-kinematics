◆ forwardKinematics()

pose universalRobots::UR::forwardKinematics ( const JointVector &  targetJointVal) const

Precondition: every joint value must be finite and in [-2π, 2π]. Throws std::invalid_argument otherwise. Note: also refreshes the joint-pose/transform cache used by operator<< and the joint-pose accessors (see the mutable members above) – callable on a const UR, but not side-effect-free with respect to that cache.
